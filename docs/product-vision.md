# Product Vision - SecondBrain

## Problem statement

People log activities inconsistently and have no consolidated view of how their time and habits affect productivity, mood, and progress.

## Product idea

SecondBrain is a digital diary plus behavioral assistant:

- Track daily activities (sleep, work, leisure, exercise, focus sessions).
- Integrate passive telemetry later (screen time, GitHub commits, calendar, wearable data).
- Use AI to generate contextual feedback and next-step suggestions.
- Add progression mechanics so users feel momentum and accomplishment.

## Target users

- Builders and knowledge workers who want clarity on routines.
- Students/professionals balancing focused work and personal life.
- Users who like quantified-self workflows but need actionable insights.

## MVP objectives (phase 1)

- Daily manual logging of core activity categories.
- Journal note entry for qualitative context.
- Dashboard-style daily/weekly summaries.
- Basic progression model (streaks, XP, milestones).
- AI-generated daily reflection comment based on logged data.

## Future objectives (phase 2+)

- Auto-ingestion: screen time, GitHub, calendar, smartwatch.
- Adaptive suggestions using historical trends.
- Personalized goals and habit coaching.
- Gamified challenges and social accountability features.

## Core principles

- Manual-first, automation-ready.
- Explainable AI suggestions (what signal drove each suggestion).
- Privacy-first by default.
- Progressive complexity (simple at start, deeper over time).
